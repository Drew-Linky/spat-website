The channel #general is easily the one I use most besides #mspa-lit, and I noticed they also have a storage issue. I have access to the stored messages and can search for antiquated pins from the beginning of the server up through the end of 2019. However, the culture was different and it seems there is a LOT to go through, and most of it appears to be either inscrutable, currently deleted, or worthless. For now, this archive only contains the ones that are immediately available in the pin list right now and through Neth's Imgur album.

Much data might be missing, which will be corrected if possible. At the very beginning of this archive is all of the pins for which I have no idea what date they even took place, which are the hardest to find. If any intrepid soul out there wishes to help me figure out who they're from and what date they happened on, please have a look at the following images and shoot me a DM on Discord (or an email, if that's your cup of tea).
