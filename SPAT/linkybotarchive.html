Linkybot Archive

Conceived of and coded by Wizard of Chaos, Linkybot is a more simplistic bot function that simply spits out any message I've sent in the HSD over the last several years. These are usually considered funny if the output just happens to coincide with the query in some way. As I'm going through and actually adding a lot of these, I think it proves to be a more valuable repository than I thought; it showcases some of our humor in situ, and may be of some interest to historians or just as a reminder of the kinds of things we used to find funny.

Both Linkybot and its younger brother Drewbot together have been used thousands of times, and so I have a threshold for what I'm willing to put in here. Messages were filtered by whether they have 10 or more reactions, or perhaps a combination of other reactions, and I've combed through the resulting list to see what gets put in the archive (my choices of what make the cut are somewhat arbitrary, if you think I've missed something important feel free to reach out to me). WoC also inserted a random chance--1 in 10,000, if I remember correctly--for Linkybot to spit out an image edit he made of me, which actually appears in this list at least once.

A huge thank you to Kaisheng21 for assistance with finding the information and thank you also to Gio for advice on how to work with the results.
