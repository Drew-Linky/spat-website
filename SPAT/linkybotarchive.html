Linkybot Archive

Conceived of and coded by Wizard of Chaos, Linkybot is a more simplistic bot function that simply spits out any message I've sent in the HSD over the last several years. These are usually considered funny if the output just happens to coincide with the query in some way.

Both Linkybot and its younger brother Drewbot together have been used thousands of times, and so I have a threshold for what I'm willing to put in here. Only messages that were deemed very funny (10 or more ) get put in the archive.
