About Me


Several People Are Typing

A detailed journal following the events and people of the Homestuck Discord Server, which might be described as a work of amateur ethnography. The core document was written across two years with new updates every so often; concluded as of the 17th of April 2026, though may experience minor updates in the future for various reasons. Main entries have a word count of approximately 543,000; user contributions including Voces Populi are 51,000 words; the "Related Materials" section is 146,000 words.



Sydlexia Retrospective

A brief, less detailed account of the Sydlexia community, written several years after the community's decline in the early 2010s. (Approx. 9000 words)
