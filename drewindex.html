Drew Linky's Projects



Several People Are Typing

A detailed journal following the events and people of the Homestuck Discord Server, which might be described as a work of amateur ethnography. The core document was written across two years with new updates every so often; be sure to check changelog within for updates. Original writing is roughly 558,000 words as of the 11th of October 2025. Attached annex with various supplemental information constitutes an additional 153,000 words.



Sydlexia Retrospective

A brief, less detailed account of the Sydlexia community, written several years after the community's decline in the early 2010s. (Approx. 9000 words)
